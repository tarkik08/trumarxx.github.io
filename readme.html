Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take in India?

Typically, the process takes 18-24 months if there are no objections. Trumarx expedites filing and promptly handles examination reports to minimize delays.

Do you handle international patent filing?

Yes. We specialize in PCT (Patent Cooperation Treaty) applications, allowing you to seek patent protection in over 150 countries simultaneously.
